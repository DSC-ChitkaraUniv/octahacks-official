FAQ

At OctaHacks, we strive to bring together developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We usually try to keep things simple. Read our FAQs to phase out ambiguity.

Why "Octahacks"?
OctaHacks is a hackathon that promotes eight tracks to innovate, ranging from local community to health and education. Possibilities are endless!!
Who can attend?
Everyone who is amazed by the changing technology and believes can solve real world problems. If you are a college/university student, you are most welcome! You can participate in teams of 2-4 students. You don’t have a team? No Problem! You can find a team fit for you here!
How to confirm my seat?
All the participants will receive an confirmation email after the registration. Students will be allowed on that basis.
What to do?
After the workshop in the first part you have to select from the 8 problem statement and have to make a Solution for the problem with maximum efficiency.
What should I bring?
Your laptop and charger. That’s it! If your project requires hardware, bring your own equipments.
Selection Criteria?
Selection will be on the basis of your GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.
How can I become a sponsor?
Awesome! Please have a look at our Sponsor Deck and Contact Us @ sponsors@octahacks.tech. Our team will get back at you.
What about food?
Food will be available at the venue at nominal charges.
Travel and Accomodation?
Unfortunately, we will not be able to provide any travel reimbursements. The accomodation will be on the venue itself.
